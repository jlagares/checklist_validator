## Bedroom Cleaning Checklist

- [ ] Have you dismantled the dressers and headboard?
- [ ] Have you washed curtains and blinds?
- [ ] Have you cleaned the windows?
- [ ] Have you organized the closet?
- [ ] Have you washed the pillows?
- [ ] Have you changed the bed linens?
- [ ] Have you flipped the mattress?
- [ ] Have you vacuumed the floor?
- [ ] Have you dusted the lamps?
- [ ] Have you dismantled and cleaned the decor?
- [ ] Have you cleaned the cabinets and shelves?
- [ ] Have you dismantled and cleaned doors and frames?
- [ ] Have you cleaned and vacuumed walls and corners?
- [ ] Have you wiped the baseboards?
