## Section 2: Office Room

- [ ] Are computers, screens, keyboards, and seats dusted?
- [ ] Are copiers, fax machines, file cabinets, and dividers dusted?
- [ ] Are pictures and clocks dusted?

## Section 3: Restroom
- [ ] Is the trash bin emptied and refilled with new liners?
- [ ] Are mirrors cleaned and polished, including the top?
- [ ] Are sinks cleaned and disinfected?
- [ ] Are toilets (including seats on both sides), urinals, wash basins, doors, handles, and partitions cleaned and disinfected?

## Section 5: Kitchen Area

- [ ] Is the coffee machine wiped clean?
- [ ] Are sinks and taps washed and disinfected?
- [ ] Are cabinets and appliances' exteriors spot cleaned?
- [ ] Is the countertop and tables cleaned and disinfected?

## Section 6: Weekly Cleaning List

- [ ] Have waste bins in all rooms been washed?
- [ ] Have light switches, light fixtures, door handles, wall clocks, and picture frames been wiped down?
- [ ] Are chairs, upholstery, drapes, and blinds vacuumed?
- [ ] Have any missing cleaning supplies been ordered?

## Section 7: Monthly Cleaning List
- [ ] Are hard floors buffed and polished?
- [ ] Are shelves, books, filing cabinets, and radiators cleaned and disinfected?
- [ ] Have outdoor windows been squeegeed?
- [ ] Are vents dusted and vacuumed?
- [ ] Are window sills dusted and washed?

## Section 8: Initial Cleaning
- [ ] Is any hard surface flooring machine scrubbed, including corners and edges?
- [ ] Have all carpets been extracted, washed clean, and replaced?
- [ ] Have all surfaces, windowsills, tables, and chairs been damp wiped?
- [ ] Is high dusting of light fixtures, air vents, and doorframes completed?
- [ ] Are all WC basins, toilets, urinals, and showers sanitized?
- [ ] Have all lime and mineral deposits in restrooms and kitchen been removed?
